Sukoon

Ghar aata hu school SE,
Pata Hai ki kapde khoonti PE taangne Hain,
Lekin phir bhi kapde kachmod kar kursi me,
Pata nai Kya sukoon milta Hai.

So kar uthta hu,
Pata Hai ki rajaai samet ka rakhni Hai,
Lekin phr bhi use asimta chod jaane me,
Pata nai Kya sukoon milta Hai.

Mai mez par padhai kar raha tha,
Maa ne bola Tha sone SE pehle kitaabe saleeke se rakh dena,
Phir bhi mez PE kitaabe avyavasthit rakhkar soo Jane me,
Pata nai Kya sukoon milta Tha.

Ab bada ho Gaya hu,
Sab kaam Ek samay saarini se karne ki sochta hu,
Lekin phr bhi Saare kaam asamay karne me,
Pata nai Kya sukoon milta Hai.

Kisi ne Ek baar mujhse kaha,
Puraani baatien yaad Karke koi faayda nai,
Lekin phr bhi purani baatein yaad Karke muskurane me,
Pata nai Kya sukoon milta Hai.

Aur phr Kisi ne mujhe bataya,
Ki apni bhawnaao ko sare aam nilaam mat Kiya karo,
Lekin phr bhi Kisi apne se bhawanaaein vyakt karne me,
Pata nai Kya sukoon milta Hai.
